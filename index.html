Who We Are

A branch of Xin Yu Sino-fortune Real Estate, committed to becoming Myanmar's premier quality life operator.

Myanmar Sino-Fortune Real Estate Development Co., Ltd is a branch of Xin Yu Sino-fortune Real Estate Co., Ltd registered in 2022, focusing on commercial real estate development and operation in Myanmar.

Leveraging the strong background of Sino-fortune Group — outstanding brand strength, innovative products and quality services — we develop projects in Myanmar with the vision of urban operation.

Following the parent company's concept of resource-saving and environment-friendly real estate, we are striving to become a prominent commercial real estate force, promoting local urban development and quality life upgrading.

We're willing to rely on the "One Belt, One Road" Initiative and the strategy of "Created in China" and "Technology Going Global" — making positive contributions to helping Myanmar build a better future.
